In a doctoral programme, a total of 180 credits shall be collected. This includes a minimum of 30 and a maximum of 50 credits for acquiring course material, and a minimum of 130 and a maximum of 150 credits for scientific research, artistic activities, or guided tutoring. A maximum of 45 credits may be collected per term, with a maximum of 30 credits in total that can be earned for tutoring during the doctoral programme. Additionally, in one term, up to forty-five credits may be earned in the training and research phase and up to sixty in the research and dissertation phase.
